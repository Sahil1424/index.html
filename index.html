How to make Grilled Chesse

Mmm,mmm,good!


Image of an grilled Chesse

Ingredients you will need:

  • 2 pieces of bread
  • 2 pats of butter
  • 1 slice of monterey jack Chesse
  • 1 slice of aged cheddar cheese

Process:

  1. Fetch yourself a fat-bottom skillet
  2. Heat the skilled over medium heat
  3. Butter one side of each slice of bread
  4. Place one slice of the buttered bread, butter side down into the skillet
  5. Quickly place each slic eof Chesse on the bread, being sure to cover the entire surface area
  6. Place the other slice of bread, butter side up, on top of the Chesse
  7. Wait approxiamtely 1 to 2 minutes (or till the bread touching the skillet is a golden brown)
  8. Flip the sandwitch over with some awesome pizzas and cook the other side till golen brown
  9. Ea twith ketchup, or just enjoy the plain crispy goodness of your grilled Chesse

Note:
Grilled chesse is great on a grill, but for quicker method, try using a clamshell panini press. The result will be much quicker, allowing you to make many more for you and your friend.

How to make French Fries
Crispy and delicious!

Ingredients:

  • 4 large patatoes
  • a pot of boiling water
  • a pot of oil heated to 350 degrees fahrenheit

Process:

  1. Cut the patotoes into strips, roughly a 1/4" by 1/4"
  2. Place the patotoes strips into the boiling water for approximately for 2 minutes
  3. Remove them and let them dry, or pat them dry
  4. Once dry, place th estrips into the hot oil
  5. Cook for approximately 8 minutes or until golden brown and crispy
  6. Serve on a plate with ketchup, vinegar, or if you're a complete weirdo, mayonnaise
